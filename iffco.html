IFFCO (Indian Farmers Fertiliser Cooperative Limited)

IFFCO, also known as the Indian Farmers Fertiliser Cooperative Limited, is a cooperative society established in 1967 with the aim of improving the economic conditions of farmers in India. Headquartered in New Delhi, IFFCO is one of the largest cooperative societies in the world. The primary objective of IFFCO is to provide farmers with high-quality fertilizers, agricultural products, and services at affordable prices. As a cooperative, IFFCO is owned and governed by the farmers themselves, making it a unique and farmer-centric organization. Over the years, IFFCO has played a crucial role in the agricultural development of India by promoting modern farming practices, providing education and training to farmers, and ensuring the availability of essential agricultural inputs. IFFCO's commitment to sustainable agriculture is reflected in its efforts to promote organic farming and environmentally friendly practices. The cooperative also actively engages in research and development to introduce innovative solutions for the farming community. With a vast network of cooperative societies and a strong presence across the country, IFFCO continues to contribute significantly to the well-being of farmers and the overall growth of the agricultural sector in India.
